üå∏ The Lotus Review System‚Ñ¢

We are pioneering a standardized approach to reviewing end-of-life care through our proprietary Lotus Review System‚Ñ¢.

This system ensures that quality care is measured consistently across all providers, giving families the confidence to make informed decisions. Each lotus flower represents a quality dimension of care:

  • Compassionate: Caring and empathetic staff
  • Responsive: Timely and attentive service
  • Professional: Skilled and knowledgeable care
  • Supportive: Emotional and practical support for families

The more qualities a provider demonstrates, the higher their rating - helping families find the best care for their loved ones.
