The Lotus Rating™ System

Like the lotus flower that brings beauty and peace, our rating system helps you find care providers who honor dignity, compassion, and comfort during life's most precious transition.

⚡ Simple & Fast! Families check which qualities they experienced. The more qualities, the more lotus flowers!

🪷
#1 Compassionate

Kind, caring, and empathetic staff who treat patients and families with dignity

🪷
#2 Responsive

Quick to respond to needs, concerns, and questions from families

🪷
#3 Supportive

Helpful guidance and support for both family and patient throughout the journey

🪷
#4 Professional

Skilled, knowledgeable, and reliable care with proper credentials and expertise

Example: If a provider is Compassionate, Responsive, and Supportive (but not Professional) → They receive 🪷🪷🪷 (3 out of 4 lotus flowers)
