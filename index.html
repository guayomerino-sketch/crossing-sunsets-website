The Lotus Rating System

Our thoughtful approach to rating care providers focuses on what matters most to families during difficult times.

🪷 Compassionate

Kind, caring, and empathetic staff who treat patients and families with dignity

🪷 Responsive

Quick to respond to needs, concerns, and questions from families

🪷 Supportive

Helpful guidance and emotional support for both patient and family members

🪷 Professional

Skilled, knowledgeable, reliable care with proper credentials and expertise
