How Crossing Sunsets™ Works

Finding the right care provider in three simple steps

🔍

1. Search & Discover

Browse verified hospice care providers, boarding care homes, and skilled nursing facilities in your area. Filter by location, services, and Lotus Rating™.

🪷

2. Read Authentic Reviews

Access genuine reviews from families who've experienced the care firsthand. Our unique Lotus Rating™ system evaluates compassion, communication, and quality of care.

3. Connect with Confidence

Reach out directly to providers knowing you've made an informed decision. Get peace of mind during a difficult time.

Our unique rating system evaluates care providers on what matters most: compassion, communication, pain management, family support, and dignity. Each lotus petal represents excellence in end-of-life care.
